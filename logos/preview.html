Logo Concepts — Lee Villarasa

Round 1 — First Set
1 — Refined LV Monogram
Geometric LV ligature where L's baseline flows into V's apex. Dark badge + gradient border. Side-by-side layout.
2 — Systems Network
5-node diamond topology with glowing gradient nodes — represents systems thinking. Technical, infrastructure-first.
3 — Architectural Blueprint
Engineering cross-section with foundation beam, pier, and lintel. Consulting-firm gravitas, premium stamp feel.
4 — Velocity Mark
L+V share a diagonal stroke forming a forward arrow. Most dynamic — evokes momentum and transformation.
Round 2 — Balanced Variations
5 — Diamond Stack
Diamond badge icon stacked above the wordmark — centered vertical layout. Most badge-like and symmetric.
6 — Circle Monogram
Perfect circle badge with equal-weight LV strokes beside a two-line stacked wordmark. Balanced and refined.
7 — Pure Wordmark
No icon — the name IS the logo. "LEE" and "VILLARASA" forced to equal width, editorial luxury feel.
8 — Orbital Precision
Circle ring with LV mark inside + precision dots at 12 and 6 o'clock. Thin vertical separator divides icon from wordmark.
